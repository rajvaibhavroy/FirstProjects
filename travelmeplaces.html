1.Leh Ladakh


Ladakh is a union territory in the Kashmir region of India. Formerly falling in the state of jammu and Kashmir, Ladakh was administered a union territory on 31st october 2019. extending from Siachen Glacier to the main great Himalayas, Ladakh is a land like no other. The rivers mountains greenery makes it very beautifull.

Best time to travel : Jun to Sep


2.Srinagar


Famously known as 'Heaven on Earth, Srinagar is located in the union territory of Jammu and Kashmir, on the bank of river Jhelum Srinagar is stationary houseboats and gandola-type rowboats- Shikaras on Dal lake.

Best time to travel : April to October


3.Manali

With spectacular valleys, breathtaking views snowcapped mountains and lush forests of oak, deodar, and pine Manali is a magical hill station at the northern end of kullu valley in Himachal pradesh. Gifted to the world by the mighty himalayas it is known to be one of the most popular destination.

Best time to travel : October to June


4.Coorg

Located amidst imposing mountains in Karnataka with a perpetually misty landscape. Coorg is popular coffee producing hill station. It is popular for its beautiful green hills and the streams cutting right through them. It also stands as a popular destination because of its people and culture. 

Best time to travel : October to March


5.Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Replete with turquoise blue water beaches and a bit of history. Andaman and Nicobar islands is a little slice of paradise tucked around 1,400 km away from the east coast of mainland India
